Level 1: The First Leap

High-Level Mechanic: Basic movement and single jump.
Detailed Mechanics Introduced: Basic horizontal movement (left/right), single jump, safe landing.
Design Elements:
Starting Area: A flat, safe area for the player to get a feel for movement without immediate threats.
Simple Platforms: A series of stationary, evenly spaced platforms requiring basic single jumps to traverse. The gaps will be small and forgiving.
Varying Heights: Platforms will introduce slight variations in height to teach players about different jump arcs.
Optional Collectible Detour (Low Skill/Low Reward): A slightly higher, short platform path off the main route leading to a single standard coin, requiring a slightly more precise single jump. This teaches players that exploring can yield minor rewards.
Objective: Reach the end of the level, possibly marked by a simple goal post or exit.
Challenges:
Getting the timing right for consecutive jumps.
Accurately landing on small platforms.
Player Learning:
Confidence in basic controls.
Understanding jump distance and height.
Difficulty Score (Estimated): 2.0
